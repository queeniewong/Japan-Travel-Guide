Kimono きもの

Kimono is the traditional Japanese Costume. Wearing Kimono is one of my wish when I travelling to Japan. I got a chance to experience the kimono wearing when I visited Kyoto in 2013.

There are many shops you can rent kimono in Kyoto. Most of them can rent for half day. You don't need to worry if you never wear kimono before. They will help you to dress up, some of them will include the hair style.

That time, I went to a well-known kimono rental shop called "Sensho-Kitamura". It was a very good experience and memory. Staffs are very nice especially the old lady who help me to wear the kimono. The price for rental kimono included the dressing, hair style and accessories like bag and sandals. It is recommended to bring small belongings with you. But if you have large belongings with you, you can leave it in the shop and get back later when you return the dress. It will be definitely a great memory for your Japan trip!
