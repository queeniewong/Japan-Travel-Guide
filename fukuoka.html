Fukuoka

Maizuru Park

I would recommend you to visit this place during late March or early April. In Japan, they will have a list of top 100 places which are the greatest place to see the cherry blossom. Maizura Park is one of top 100 places. Also, inside the park, there is the Fukuoka Castle. You can enjoy the castle under cherry blossom!
