Kyoto

Kiyomizu Temple

Kiyomizudera is one of the famous spot in Kyoto. It is well known because of its wooden stage which is 13 meters above the hill. You can enjoy the wonderful scenery there. It is a very good place to enjoy the cherry blossom and red leaves during Spring and Autumn.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Fushimi Inari Shrine is a Shinto shrine located at Mount Inari in southern Kyoto. In the past, Mount Inari is a holy place. Since ancient times,Fushimi Inari Shrine is mainly for worship the Inari God and agriculture use.

Starting from Edo period, Kyoto people has a custom. Peolpe will donate a torii gate to show the respect of god. Therefore, there are thousands of torii gates around the Fushimi Inari Shrine. Also, it is famous of "Thousands of Torii Gate" around the world.

I am in love with this place because of the torii gate. When you walk along, it is just like walking inside a red tunnel. It is really a beautiful place which you must go once! Unfortunately, I did not have a chance to walk through the whole torii gate route last time. I will definitely go here again in the future.

As those torii gates were built along the Mount Inari, it leads to the wooden forest. It is recommended to go there in the morning. Otherwise, it might be scary to walk under the torri gate at night. Also, the whole torii gate route is around 2 kilometers and takes you 2 hours to finish it. For those would like to visit it, you should estimate your visiting time carefully.

Enjoy the leisure walk under thousands torii gates!
