Ramen　ラーメン

I believed most of you have tried Ramen at least one time. But have you ever think that you can make ramen yourself one day?

Ramen is a kind of noodles which is one of the popular food in Japan. Japanese will eat ramen together with fried dumplings. In the trip to Kyushu, I found this ramen school by chance. This Ramen school is opened by a famous Ramen restaurant called "IPPUDO [一風堂]". Although this school is called "Child Kitchen", adults are also welcomed to join the lesson. During the lesson, you can learn and experience how ramen and dumplings are made. Following the teacher, you will cook your own ramen and fried your dumplings. Finally, you can enjoy the meal with the classmates.

Note:
(1) Teacher can speak only Japanese, but she is nice and will teach you patiently.
(2) Please make reservation before you go. Reservation is available two months before your desire date.
